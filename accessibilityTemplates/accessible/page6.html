Responsibilities

  • Assist in the design, day-to-day management and delivery of client analyses
  • Collaborate with client to identify objectives and develop requirements and specifications
  • Manage internal team to design, develop and deliver project deliverables
  • Ensure that client deliverables are produced on time and error-free
  • Occasional travel

Requirements

  • 2 -5 years of experience in project management, data analysis or software development.
  • Demonstrated ability to interpret analytic output.
  • Strong attention to detail.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
  • Experience working in a team-oriented, deadline driven environment
